Here's a timeline of Grace Jones Life

  • Life
  • 1948: Grace Jones is born in Spanish Town, Jamaica.
  • 1965: Jones moves to New York City with her family.
  • 1967: She attends Syracuse University on scholarship and studies theatre.
  • 1970: Jones begins her career as a model.
  • 1973: She makes her film debut in "Gordon's War."
  • 1977: Jones signs with Island Records and releases her debut album, "Portfolio."
  • 1980: She stars in the film "Trouble in Mind."
  • 1981: Jones releases the album "Nightclubbing," featuring hits such as "Pull Up to the Bumper."
  • 1983: She appears in the James Bond film "Octopussy."
  • 1985: Jones plays the Bond villainess May Day in "A View to a Kill" and releases the album "Slave to the Rhythm."
  • 1986: She stars in the film "Vamp."
  • 1987: Jones appears in the film "Straight to Hell."
  • 1992: She stars in the Eddie Murphy film "Boomerang" and releases the album "Bulletproof Heart."
  • 1998: Jones plays the role of Calypso in "McCinsey's Island."
  • 2003: She appears in the documentary "Grace Jones: Live in Concert."
  • 2008: Jones is the subject of the documentary "Grace Jones: Bloodlight and Bami."
  • 2011: She performs at the Diamond Jubilee Concert for Queen Elizabeth II.
  • 2015: Jones publishes her autobiography, "I'll Never Write My Memoirs."
  • 2017: She releases the album "Bloodlight and Bami - The Remixes."
